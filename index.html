About Simplix

Simplix is a Custom ROM based on Cosmic OS 3.2 Pulsar with additional features, performance and battery life improvements.

It also brings simplicity to your phone. Ever wanted to flash a Custom ROM and see good aesthetics everywhere without touching a setting? Simplix makes it easy to flash and forget. We constantly try to add simple but effective conviniences everywhere to make your experience even better over time.

Of course, everybody has a different taste. That's why we provide flexibility. You can turn on and off every additional extra we provide, just in case it doesn't work out great for you.

Whatever you do - your experience can always be better. That's why we accept feature requests over at our Official Telegram Group.


Our latest release, based on Android 8.1 Oreo is called Simplix Orange.
